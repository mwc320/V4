Simple Payment Scheduling

Workspace projects are organized by payment milestones just like they would be on a typical contract. When a milestone is marked as complete, you get paid - simple as that.

Milestone-Based Payments

Milestone-based payments provide an additional layer of security for projects as well as a transparent payment schedule for clients.

Low-Cost, Transparent Fees

Receiving a credit card payment costs 2.9% + 30¢ per charge and ACH transfers have a $5 flat fee.
We'll never take a percentage of your job.

Seamless & Secure Payments

Streamlined Communication

Forget playing phone-tag or navigating endless strings of emails and text messages. With Workspace, you can quickly send group or direct messages to project collaborators - in real time.
